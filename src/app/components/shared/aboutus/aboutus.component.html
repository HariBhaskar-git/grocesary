Our journey and our team..

Grocery dunia has been established in 2021 by 3 friends who believed that the packaged food industry in our country needs food to be served with heart and not just the mind. We learned while undertaking this journey as newbabies in this crazy sector.

  • Grocery dunia is committed to add quality to your life with the quality food and other dailyneed products.
  • We promise you that grocery dunia provides only best and quality organic products.

Today we are still a work in progress as most things in life. We love the idea of collobarations and working with the one who works from heart while using their mind to compliment it.

Our mission..

  • To promote organic (chemical free) and natural (whole, unrefined, unadulterated) foods in order to keep the earth and her people beauty.
  • Encourage small farmers, organic farming and small manufacturers.
  • Promote eco-friendly and sustainable products with a great responsibility to protect environment with best price.
